Our Vision

Our vision is to equip and train pastors who are passionate about planting churches, reaching communities, and fulfilling the Great Commission with boldness and biblical integrity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide sound biblical and theological education that prepares men and women for effective ministry through intensive training in Scripture, doctrine, leadership, and evangelism.

Our Legacy

Founded in 1981 and operating faithfully for over four decades, Bethel Faith Spark Bible College stands as a beacon of theological training in South India. Our alumni serve across the nation and globe, planting churches and leading ministries with excellence and integrity.
