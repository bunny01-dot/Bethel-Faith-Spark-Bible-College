Admission Process

We welcome applications from dedicated Christian men and women who are called to serve God through theological education and ministry.

  • Admissions open year-round for all courses.
  • Students can apply through both offline and online modes.
  • Application forms are available at the college office and will be soon available online.
  • Interviews and pastoral recommendations are required as part of the selection process.

Eligibility

  • Certificate in Theology – 9th Pass
  • Diploma in Theology – 10th Pass
  • Bachelor of Theology – 12th Pass
